Who We Are

We are a small kiosk inside a Safeway in Peoples Plaza, Delaware. Five young, determined adults work together as a team to keep their kiosk clean and successful in Safeway's busy grocery store environment. Our kiosk is just like a regular Starbucks -- only smaller but mightier! We sell coffee, lattes, teas, frappuccinos, pastries, tumblers, and much more! If you would like to learn more about what we sell, then click here.

Did You Get Your Club Card?

With your Club Card, you can enjoy exclusive savings all across the store AND anytime you shop online at Safeway.com. Safeway Club Card membership entitles you to enjoy all our special money-saving promotions. Your savings are automatically deducted and appear on your receipt. If you forget your Card, just enter your telephone number at checkout and receive your savings. If you would like to sign up, please click here and hand the completed application to a Safeway cashier or to a Safeway in-store customer service.
